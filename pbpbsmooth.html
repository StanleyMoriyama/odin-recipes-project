Nutritious Protein Peanut Butter Smoothie

Description of Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk, or alternative milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps to make

  1. Place all ingredients inside a blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
